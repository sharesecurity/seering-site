Threadlytics
Thread mesh analytics for iOS

Threadlytics is an iOS app that gives you a living graph of your smart home. It discovers Thread border routers and mesh networks, correlates devices across Apple Home, Home Assistant, SmartThings, and other ecosystems, and shows you exactly how your devices communicate — from the application layer down to the radio link.

About Seering

Seering LLC is a software company focused on smart home network intelligence. We believe that as homes get smarter, the people living in them deserve tools to understand what's happening beneath the surface.

Our first product, Threadlytics, was born from the simple frustration of not being able to see how Thread mesh networks, Matter devices, and smart home ecosystems actually work together — and what to do when they don't.
